Pricing FAQ

What counts as a measurement?

Each completed distance, angle, area, path, diameter, or coordinate result counts toward the free daily limit.

When does the free limit reset?

The free counter resets each day. If you reach the cap, the app shows the remaining time until the next reset.

Do I need Pro to use exports or project files?

No. The paid difference is usage volume: Pro removes the daily measurement cap.
